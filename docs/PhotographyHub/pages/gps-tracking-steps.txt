# Prepare GPSMAP 67
- Go outside where GPS can lock satellites.
- Confirm recording interval (e.g., 1s) and accuracy mode.
- Verify time and time zone.

# Record the activity
- Start activity on GPSMAP 67.
- Walk/drive your route; check track is recording.
- End and save the activity; give it a clear name.

# Sync & export GPX
- Sync to iPhone (Garmin Explore / Connect).
- Export .GPX.
- AirDrop to Mac (or copy to SanDisk).
